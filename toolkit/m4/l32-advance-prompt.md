# Advanced Prompting

Providing additional context is the most effective way to obtain accurate, relevant results.

## Adding Context
In this section we introduce three advanced techniques to enrich your prompts with more information.

### 1. Examples
Including examples of the desired output clarifies the type of response you expect. You can paste an existing answer (e.g., a job description, a report, or a survey questionnaire) directly into the prompt or upload it as a file. **Remember to tell the Gen‑AI tool that you are providing an example.**

### 2. Personas
Ask the Gen‑AI to adopt a specific persona (role or character) to inject richer context about the output you need. Personas help focus the model on particular information, tone, or style. For instance, you could request the model to act as a Chief Marketing Officer, a McKinsey Senior Partner, a 32‑year‑old mom from Chicago, or even Steve Jobs.

### 3. Tone
Adjusting the tone changes the style and manner of the response. Use this to make an email more professional, or to experiment with different communication styles (motivational, reassuring, serious, etc.).

## Prompting Tips
- **Keep language clear, simple, and specific.**
- **Break down complex tasks** into smaller prompts. This makes troubleshooting easier. If you combine steps, give clear instructions like “first do X, then do Y”.
- **Rephrase** if the model doesn't understand. Try saying it differently, just as you would with a person.
