# Lesson 30 of 49 – Module 4: Introduction

> **Overview**: By now, you’ve explored the capabilities and applications of Generative AI. This module shifts focus to hands‑on practice, empowering you to craft effective prompts, manage risks, and partner with AI responsibly.

## What You’ll Learn

- **Prompt Engineering** – Design clear, well‑structured instructions that guide Gen AI to produce optimal results.
- **Contextual Enhancements** – Add examples, personas, and tone to shape outputs.
- **Risk Management** – Identify and mitigate bias, inaccuracies, and privacy concerns.
- **Human‑in‑the‑Loop** – Leverage Gen AI as a problem‑solving collaborator while retaining critical judgment.

## Module Activities

1. **Craft Strong Prompts** – Hands‑on exercises to refine prompt clarity and relevance.
2. **Add Context** – Practice using examples, personas, and tone adjustments.
3. **Recognize Risks** – Spot bias, verify accuracy, and protect sensitive data.
4. **Apply Judgement** – Use AI to augment decision‑making without replacing human insight.

By the end of this module, you’ll be equipped to use Gen AI thoughtfully, creatively, and responsibly, driving better outcomes in your work and daily life.
